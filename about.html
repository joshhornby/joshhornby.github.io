About

I'm Josh, a Principal Engineer working at Journey, leading the ecommerce teams in helping hotels generate more revenue.

I've worked at cinch, ChooseMyCar and a few other places.

This blog is where I share lessons learned, both to help others and to keep improving my own ability to share them. Most posts will draw directly from my work, mixing technical insights with themes on engineering leadership.

If you have any questions about my writing, then don't hesitate to email josh [at] joshhornby.co.uk - I'd love to hear from you.
